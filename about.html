About Us

Wood Shift started as an idea to bring the best furniture designs to homes in a cost effective and timely manner. Launched in 2022, it has quickly become a favourite destination for buying furniture and home decor products.

While traditional furniture vendors can't factor customization into their products, our products can be custom built to match the customer's requirements. All this on top of a super speedy logistic system gives us a furniture shopping experience like never before.

We have a robust support team that is ready to help our customers whenever required. From home surveys for installations to the materials used in the products, everything is done as per their satisfaction.

We offer hundreds of designs for modular kitchens, countertops, cabinets and more. Check out our gallery for the entire product range.
